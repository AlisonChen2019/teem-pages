Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/17/2026

Groop is an iMessage app extension that helps users schedule events directly within Messages. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

What We Collect

When you use Groop to create or join an event, we collect and store the following on our servers:

  • Event name — the name you give the event
  • Display name — the user name you enter
  • Conversation-scoped identifier — a random, opaque identifier tied to your use of the app within a specific Messages conversation. It is not your Apple ID, phone number, email address, or real name, and cannot be reversed to reveal any of these. The same person will have a different identifier in a different conversation or if messaging through a different app.

We do not require account creation, login, email address, or phone number.

How We Use This Data

This data is used solely to operate the core event scheduling functionality of the app — for example, associating an event with its participants and displaying who has responded.

Data Retention

Event data is deleted from our servers 90 days after event is created.]

Data Not Collected

We do not collect your real name, contacts, location, photos, or device identifiers such as IDFA. We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party trackers.

Third Parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data is stored using Supabase, which processes data on our behalf under their own security and privacy practices.]

Your Choices

You can request deletion of your event data at any time by contacting us at groopsupport@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Groop is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions or data deletion requests: groopsupport@gmail.com
