How to Use Groop

  1. Open a conversation in Messages
  2. Tap the App Store icon in the Messages app drawer and select Groop
  3. Enter an event name and set the date and time windows
  4. Send the message to schedule the event with your group
  5. Tap on the sent message to add your specific availability and name

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?
No — Groop doesn't require sign-up or login.

Is my data stored anywhere?
Yes — event names, display names, and a random identifier scoped to your conversation are stored on our servers to make the scheduling feature work. See our Privacy Policy for details.
